ABOUT UPTOWN URBANIA

DISCOVER THANE’S FIRST ROOFTOP RECREATION ZONE ON THE 47TH FLOOR..

Welcome to Uptown Urbania, a world co-created by architects, behaviourists, child psychologists, environmentalists, sunlight experts, interior designers, sculptors, artists and landscape designers. A multidisciplinary approach we like to call design thinking. Where 55 acres become a magical 60 acres of friendships, laughter and family time. The first cluster of towers at Uptown Urbania, La Familia, feature 2 and 3 bedroom homes. The 3 multi-storeyed towers with 45 habitable floors are thoughtfully designed from head to toe. It's a placewith a clubhouse with outdoor amenities on the ground floor. A place where the 46th and 47th floor are leisure zones not just restricted to a privileged few but open to the entire community. Replete with a rooftop swimming pool, sky gym, sunset terrace, rooftop bar and stargazing deck. Think of it as a human-centric experience dedicated to everything the city had forgotten about life.
